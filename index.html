About Us:

A Delicious History

Welcome to Sweet Life, where passion meets indulgence. We are dedicated to creating delicious and indulgent ice cream flavors that will satisfy your cravings and delight your taste buds.

2014

The Sweet Life brand is founded, dedicated to creating delicious and indulgent ice cream flavors. The founder, Ms.Bianco, combine her passion for dessert-making and her love for quality ingredients to start her journey.

2016

Sweet Life opens its first flagship store in the heart of a bustling city, offering a wide range of unique and mouthwatering ice cream flavors. The store quickly gains popularity among locals and tourists alike, becoming a go-to destination for ice cream lovers.

2020

Sweet Life expands its reach by launching an online store, allowing customers from all over the country to enjoy their delectable ice cream flavors. The brand's commitment to using high-quality ingredients and innovative recipes continues to attract a loyal following.

2023

Sweet Life celebrates its 10th anniversary with a series of special events and limited-edition flavors. The brand's success and commitment to delivering exceptional ice cream experiences have made it a beloved name in the industry, earning numerous awards and accolades.
